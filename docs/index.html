Paper Graph

Interactive map of AI & security research papers

  • Nodes are papers — sized by interest score (1–10), colored by tag: Security, Cyber, General
  • Edge layers (toolbar toggle):
    • Citations — who cites whom
    • Shared Authors — papers by the same authors
    • Semantic — embedding similarity + topic clusters
  • Semantic mode rearranges nodes by meaning and shows topic regions — click a label to filter
  • View modes ( ⊟ ☰ buttons): Graph only, Split (graph + sortable table), or List only. The table and graph stay in sync — clicking or hovering in one highlights the other
  • Click a node or table row to open its detail card (score, authors, summary, links)
  • Hover a node to highlight its neighbors
  • Drag a node — its neighbors follow with spring physics
  • Sort the table by title, score, tag, or date (click column headers)
  • Search papers by title from the toolbar — filters both graph and table
  • Zoom/pan with scroll wheel and mouse drag
  • Trends button (📈) opens a timeline of topic volume and a parallel chart of trending title keywords — hover streams/lines for weekly counts, click a topic to filter by cluster or a keyword to search the graph
